fifth west is located on the fifth floor of the west parallel of the east campus dormitory here at mit.

37 undergraduate students and 1 grt live on this co-ed, cats-allowed, no-smoking, clothing-not-optional (except for Wood) hall.

the hall is split into three physical sections: munroe, hayden, and wood. there are two lounges, the hayden projector lounge and the wood kitchen lounge.

this site was designed by leee (with inspiration from dove) and is maintained by the 5west tech and computer strike team (5west-tech@mit.edu).
